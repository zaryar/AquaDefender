About AquaDefender

Welcome to AquaDefender, the thrilling survival and escape game where you must build a ship to flee from an island infested with pirates. Can you survive the waves and craft your way to freedom?

Gameplay

In AquaDefender, you'll face wave-based challenges as you gather resources, craft items, and upgrade your defenses. The goal is to construct your ship and escape from the pirate-infested island.
